## Code and Insight Challenge Hackathon

<div class="de-news-section">
  <div>
    <h3>Software and data in action</h3>
    <p>Fifteen Software and Data apprentices joined the first Software and Data themed hackathon at BPP's London Portsoken campus.</p>
    <p>Working with realistic banking datasets, mixed teams cleaned data, built dashboards, created front-end views, tested their work, and presented their findings to peers and judges.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="de-news-action-card">
    <h3>Skills shown</h3>
    <ul>
      <li>Data cleaning and analysis.</li>
      <li>Dashboarding and visual storytelling.</li>
      <li>Front-end development and testing.</li>
      <li>Collaboration, communication, and presenting.</li>
    </ul>
  </div>
</div>
