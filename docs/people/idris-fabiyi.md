## Biography

Idris Fabiyi is the Head of Innovation and Technology across BPP School of Technology, Estio, and Firebrand Training. Bringing over 20 years of experience in tech and education to the forefront of digital transformation, he leverages a background in full-stack development, cloud infrastructure, and AI to bridge the gap between high-level tech architecture and hands-on learning.

Idris approaches technology not just as a builder, but as an educator dedicated to making the complex accessible and actionable for all. He drives the creation of innovative learning platforms and compelling content for the next generation of digital professionals, and is the host of The Digital Edge podcast, TechSphere. Idris is driven by a passion for problem-solving, future-proofing careers, and inspiring meaningful change across the tech community, scaling impact from the UK to the global stage.
