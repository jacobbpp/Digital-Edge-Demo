# Privacy and Analytics Notice

Last updated: 30 June 2026

Digital Edge is a public learning resource site. This notice explains the basic privacy and analytics behaviour for this website.

## Analytics

Digital Edge uses Google Analytics on the live GitHub Pages site to understand broad usage patterns, such as which pages are visited and how people move around the site.

Analytics is only loaded when the site is served from the live hostname, `jacobbpp.github.io`. It is not loaded when running the site locally.

Google Analytics may collect information such as:

- Page views and navigation paths.
- Browser and device information.
- Approximate location derived from network information.
- Referring pages or links.

Digital Edge does not use this analytics setup to intentionally identify individual learners, apprentices, staff, or visitors.

## Cookies and Third Parties

Google Analytics may use cookies or similar technologies when the live site is visited.

The site also links to third-party services, including Spotify and external learning resources. Those services have their own privacy notices and cookie practices.

## Local Development

When the site is run locally for editing or review, analytics is not loaded. Local page views are not sent to Google Analytics by this site.

## Content and Contact

Digital Edge content is provided for learning and discovery. If a page includes an outdated event, broken link, or privacy concern, it should be reviewed and corrected by the site maintainers.

This page is intended as a practical transparency notice for Digital Edge. It should be read alongside any wider organisational privacy notices that apply to BPP learners, apprentices, staff, and visitors.
